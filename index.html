About Us

BARNAGAR STUDY POINT

Barnagar Study Point is a premier educational institution located on National Highway, Sorbhog, Assam, dedicated to nurturing students from preschool through postgraduate levels. With experienced, energetic, and hardworking teachers like Rupam Nath and Bhaskar Jyoti Chaliha, we provide personalized academic guidance that goes far beyond the classroom. Our commitment to quality education has earned us a perfect 5-star reputation in the community.

At Barnagar Study Point, we believe in shaping well-rounded individuals equipped for today's competitive world. Our programs include spoken English, handwriting improvement, personality development, special Sunday classes, and career counseling — ensuring every student walks out more confident, skilled, and ready for the future. Whether you are a young learner or an adult seeking growth, our doors are open six days a week to support your journey.
